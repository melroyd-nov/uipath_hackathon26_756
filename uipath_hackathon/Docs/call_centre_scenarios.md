# Acme Insurance — Call Centre Scenarios
> Training scripts for audio clip generation. Each scenario includes intent tag, agent name, and a realistic customer interaction.
> All scenarios are under the five core intents: Cancellations | Grievances | Claims Status | Billing | New Business

## Scenario 1: Cancellation Risk and Retention
**Intent:** Cancellations

**Customer:** Hi, I received my renewal notice and the premium increased too much. I want to cancel my policy completely.

**Agent (Mike):** I understand you are looking to cancel, and I can process that for you. For data protection, could you please state your full name, NRIC, and date of birth?

**Customer:** Sure, I am David Chen. My NRIC is S1234567D, and my date of birth is January 5th, 1985.

**Agent (Mike):** Thank you, Mr. Chen. Identity verified. Before I finalize the cancellation, we highly value your loyalty. I can offer an immediate 15% retention discount on your premium if you choose to stay.

**Customer:** Oh, a 15% discount? In that case, let's keep the policy active. I'll accept that offer.

**Agent (Mike):** Wonderful. I will apply the discount right now and email you the updated policy documents. Have a great day.
